Asaba Jumah

Ugandan Actor

Biography

Asaba Jumah is a Ugandan actor known for his work in independent film productions and Wakaliwood projects. Born on September 23, 2005 in Hoima, Uganda, he has built his career through roles in action and drama films.

He gained recognition through films like Kyenvu and November Tear, and later appeared in The Girl in the Yellow Jumper and Once Upon a Time in Uganda.

He continues to represent a new generation of Ugandan actors contributing to the growth of the local film industry.
