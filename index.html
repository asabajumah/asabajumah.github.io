Asaba Jumah

Ugandan Actor

Biography

Asaba Jumah is a Ugandan actor known for his work in independent films and Wakaliwood productions. Born on September 23, 2005 in Hoima, Uganda, he has built his career through roles in action and drama films.

He has appeared in several Ugandan productions and continues to grow within the local and international film industry.
