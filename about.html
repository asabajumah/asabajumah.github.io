Asaba Jumah

Actor | Media Personality

Biography

Asaba Jumah is a Ugandan actor and media personality involved in film production and independent cinema projects.

Born on 23 September 2005 in Hoima, Uganda, he began his career in entertainment at a young age and gained recognition through film roles and media involvement.

He is known for his role in Attack on Nyege Nyege Island (2016) as Kato, and work in production and sound editing in independent films.

He continues to grow his career in acting and media production.
