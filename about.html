Asaba Jumah

Actor

Biography

Asaba Jumah is a Ugandan actor involved in film production. He is known for his contributions to independent entertainment productions in Uganda.

Born on 23 September 2005 in Uganda, Asaba Jumah began his journey in the entertainment industry at a young age and gained recognition through participation in independent film projects.

He is known for his acting role in Attack on Nyege Nyege Island (2016) as Kato, and has also contributed to behind-the-scenes production work including sound editing on Who Killed Captain Alex? 2.

Asaba Jumah continues to develop his career in acting and media work within Uganda’s entertainment industry.
